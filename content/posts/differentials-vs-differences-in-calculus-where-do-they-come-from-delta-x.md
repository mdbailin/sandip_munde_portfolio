# Differentials in Calculus: the story of $dx$ vs $\delta x$.

When you're evaluating an integral, say finding the area under some function $f(x)$ from $x=a$ to $x=b$, the first thing you write is the integral expression with the usual notations and conventions - something like: $\int\_a^b f(x) dx$.
